Our Story

A Hidden Gem in the
Heart of Hillcrest

Tucked away within the prestigious Emberton Estate, Ray's Kitchen offers an escape into a world of relaxed luxury. We believe that dining should be an immersive experience—a celebration of craft, warmth, and timeless elegance.

Our culinary philosophy is rooted in sourcing the finest local ingredients and transforming them with artistry and fire. From our signature slow-roasted pork belly to expertly crafted cocktails, every detail is designed to make you feel at home, yet entirely transported.
